Chris' Steamed Buns
-------------------
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon baking powder

Directions:
6. Divide dough into 2 parts, and place the piece you are not working with in a covered bowl. Divide each half into 12 parts.
9. Place steam-plate on a small wire rack in the middle of the wok.
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

2. Mix in 1/2 cup warm water, flour, salt, 2 tablespoons sugar, and vegetable oil.
13. Continue steaming batches of buns until all are cooked.
1 tablespoon active dry yeast
1/4 teaspoon salt

5. Sprinkle baking powder evenly on surface, and knead for 5 minutes.
10. Transfer as many buns on wax paper as will comfortably fit onto steam-plate leaving 1 to 2 inches between the buns. At least 2 inches space should be left between steam-plate and the wok.
